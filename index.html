Quick Routine

      How to Use

      This page lets you quickly check off a list of tasks — perfect for routines you repeat multiple times a day like heading out or setting up.

      Once all items are checked, the list resets and the page auto-closes. Data is saved locally on your device only.

      If you're switching devices, export and re-import your checklist using the gear menu.
